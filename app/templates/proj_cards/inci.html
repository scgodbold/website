Incident Chat Bot Zappos
A fault resistent jabber bot, that notifys, monitors, and documents progress of incidents as they occur in the Zappos enviroment. The bot also would email out interested parties to the incidents frequent updates to keep the company communication up and reduce downtime.
Technologies Used
  • Python (Flask / Celery)
  • Jabber
  • Amazon AWS (EC2 / ELB)
  • RabbitMQ
  • Jira API
